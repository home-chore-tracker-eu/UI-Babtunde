ABOUT US

MineChore is an application that makes it possible for parents to turn household chores from "bore" to "score! We are trying to help parents overcome the struggle of getting their kids to consistently and meaningfully contribute to the household chores. Easily create a list of chores (or use one of our templates) and then randomly assign them to your children. Foster healthy competition and habits with ChoreScore and CleanStreaks: our proprietary chore rating and tracking tools that have been (un)scientifically proven to be 100% more effective than side-eye and time-outs.

WHY CHOOSE MINECHORE?

Create accountability with MineChore - never again find that 6 month old lost banana rotting behind your precious little angel's nightstand. By requiring a photo of the completed chore to be uploaded, you decide if that chore is actually completed or not.
